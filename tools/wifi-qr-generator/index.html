How to Use WiFi QR Codes

Easy Guest Access

Guests can connect to your WiFi network instantly by scanning the QR code with their phone's camera app. No need to manually type long passwords or search for the network.

Security Benefits

Share WiFi access without revealing your actual password. The QR code contains encrypted network information that devices can use to connect automatically.

Compatible Devices

Works with most modern smartphones and tablets. iOS devices (iOS 11+) and Android devices can scan WiFi QR codes directly from the camera app or WiFi settings.

Business Use

Perfect for restaurants, cafes, hotels, and offices. Print the QR code on cards, posters, or table tents to provide seamless WiFi access to customers and visitors.
