How to Use Your Sitemap
1. Upload to Your Website

Save the generated sitemap as sitemap.xml and upload it to your website's root directory.

2. Submit to Search Engines

Submit your sitemap URL to:

  • Google: Google Search Console
  • Bing: Bing Webmaster Tools
  • Yandex: Yandex Webmaster
3. Add to Robots.txt

Add this line to your robots.txt file:

Sitemap: https://yourwebsite.com/sitemap.xml
4. Best Practices
  • Update your sitemap when you add new pages
  • Use appropriate priority values (0.1 to 1.0)
  • Set realistic change frequencies
  • Keep sitemaps under 50,000 URLs
