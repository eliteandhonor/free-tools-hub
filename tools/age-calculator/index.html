How Age is Calculated

Our age calculator uses precise date arithmetic to determine your exact age:

  • Years: Complete years since birth
  • Months: Total months lived including partial months
  • Days: Total days from birth date to current date
  • Hours: Days multiplied by 24 hours
  • Minutes: Hours multiplied by 60 minutes
