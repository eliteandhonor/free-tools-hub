About BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight to categorize them into health-related weight ranges.

How BMI is Calculated

  • Metric: BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height (m)²
  • Imperial: BMI = (weight (lbs) ÷ height (inches)²) × 703

BMI Limitations

  • Does not distinguish between muscle and fat mass
  • May not be accurate for athletes with high muscle mass
  • Age and gender are not considered in the calculation
  • Different ethnic groups may have different health risks at the same BMI
  • Not suitable for pregnant women, children, or elderly

When to Consult a Healthcare Professional

  • If your BMI indicates you are underweight or obese
  • Before starting any weight loss or gain program
  • If you have concerns about your weight and health
  • For personalized nutrition and exercise advice
  • If you have underlying health conditions

Healthy Lifestyle Tips

  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables
  • Engage in regular physical activity (150+ minutes per week)
  • Stay hydrated and limit processed foods
  • Get adequate sleep (7-9 hours per night)
  • Manage stress through healthy coping strategies
  • Regular health check-ups and monitoring
