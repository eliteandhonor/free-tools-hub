Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our tools and services.

Last updated: January 19, 2025

Effective date: January 19, 2025

Privacy Highlights

Local Processing

All tools process your data locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to our servers.

No Data Collection

We don't collect, store, or share your personal data or tool inputs.

Anonymous Usage

You can use all our tools without creating an account or providing personal information.

Minimal Cookies

We only use essential cookies for basic website functionality.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Tool Data

When you use our tools (such as word counter, JSON formatter, or calculators), all processing happens entirely in your browser. We do not receive, collect, or store any of the data you input into our tools.

1.2 Analytics Data

We may collect anonymous usage analytics to understand how our tools are used and to improve our services. This includes:

  • Page views and tool usage statistics
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and screen resolution
  • Referring website
  • Time spent on pages

This data is aggregated and anonymous - we cannot identify individual users.

1.3 Contact Information

If you contact us through our contact form or email, we collect only the information you provide (name, email, message) to respond to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Information

2.1 Service Provision

We use the minimal data we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our tools and website
  • Improve user experience and tool functionality
  • Respond to support requests and feedback
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns

2.2 Legal Compliance

We may process data as required by law or to protect our rights and the rights of others.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Client-Side Processing

All tool operations are performed in your browser using JavaScript. Your data never leaves your device when using our tools.

3.2 Server Data

The only data stored on our servers includes:

  • Website files and code
  • Anonymous analytics data
  • Contact form submissions (temporarily, for response purposes)

3.3 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Secure server configurations
  • Regular security updates
  • Limited data collection to minimize risk

4. Cookies and Tracking

4.1 Essential Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for basic website functionality, such as:

  • Remembering your preferences (theme, language)
  • Maintaining tool settings during your session
  • Ensuring proper website functionality

4.2 Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics cookies to understand how our website is used. These cookies:

  • Are anonymous and cannot identify you personally
  • Help us improve our tools and user experience
  • Can be disabled in your browser settings

4.3 No Advertising Cookies

We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking across other websites.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 CDN Services

We use Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) for fonts and icons to improve loading speed. These services may collect basic usage data.

5.2 Analytics Services

We may use privacy-focused analytics services to understand website usage. These services are configured to respect user privacy.

5.3 No Social Media Tracking

We do not embed social media tracking pixels or third-party advertising scripts.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Control

Since we don't collect personal data from tool usage, there's no personal data to access, modify, or delete regarding tool usage.

6.2 Contact Data

If you've contacted us, you can:

  • Request a copy of your contact data
  • Request deletion of your contact information
  • Update your contact preferences

6.3 Cookie Control

You can:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Clear existing cookies at any time
  • Use our tools without accepting cookies (basic functionality may be limited)

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

Our website can be accessed globally. If you're outside the United States, please note that any information collected may be processed in the United States, where our servers are located.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Post updates on this page
  • Update the "Last modified" date
  • Notify users of significant changes through our website

Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@freetoolshub.com

Contact Form: /contact/

Summary

Free Tools Hub is designed with privacy in mind. All our tools work entirely in your browser, meaning your data stays on your device. We collect minimal, anonymous usage data to improve our services and don't use advertising trackers or invasive analytics. You can use all our tools without creating an account or providing personal information.
